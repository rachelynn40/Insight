About radiation detection...

For those who work or live around radiation, it is especially important to track & understand the levels of radiation they are exposed to. To understand this, a wide variety of detectors are used. The science behind radiation detection has evolved & improved over time. The wide range of capabilities for detectors, plus other factors such as size & cost influence how scientists choose the detector that's best for them.

Development of Insight Data Visualization

Insight Data Visualization makes use of Python (pandas, matplotlib, becquerel, numpy), & html, css, plus javascript via Bootstrap 4 to create a freely accessible, open source tool to learn about nuclear energy, analyze nuclear spectra & manipulate data!
